RETURNS & EXCHANGES

We want you to be satisfied with your order. Our returns policy is straightforward:

  • ◆ Unused items: Unused, uninstalled items in original packaging may be returned within 14 days of delivery for a full refund.
  • ◆ Custom orders: Custom and personalised items are non-refundable unless they arrive faulty or incorrectly produced.
  • ◆ How to initiate: Email returns@griffixracing.com.au with your order number and reason. We'll provide a return address promptly.
  • ◆ Return shipping: Customer pays return postage unless the item is faulty or we sent the wrong product.
  • ◆ Refund timeline: Refunds are issued within 5 business days of receiving and inspecting the return.

DAMAGED OR WRONG ITEMS

If your order arrives damaged in transit or we've sent the wrong item, we'll make it right immediately — no arguments, no hassle.

Email us within 7 days of delivery with your order number and photos of the damage or incorrect item. We'll resend the correct kit at no cost to you and arrange collection or disposal of the original at our expense.

Damaged or wrong items: contact@griffixracing.com.au — we reply within one business day.
