Information about how Griffix Racing uses cookies and local storage on this website.

Last updated: February 2026. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which ones we use, and how you can control them.

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that a website places in your browser when you visit. They allow the site to remember information about your visit — such as which items are in your cart — so you don't have to re-enter that information every time you navigate between pages.

Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide a better user experience. They are not programs and cannot carry viruses or install malware on your device.

2. COOKIES WE USE

Griffix Racing uses a minimal, privacy-respecting approach to browser storage. We do not use advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies of any kind.

Essential — Cart Data (localStorage)

Purpose: Stores your shopping cart contents between page visits so items are not lost when you navigate away.

Storage type: Browser localStorage (not a cookie — stored locally on your device only, never sent to our servers).

Data stored: Product IDs, quantities, and selected options for items in your cart.

Duration: Persists until you clear your browser storage or complete your order.

We do not set cookies for analytics, advertising, remarketing, social media tracking, or any other purpose beyond the cart functionality described above.

3. THIRD-PARTY COOKIES

Some third-party services embedded in or used by this website may set their own cookies or cached resources for performance purposes. Griffix Racing has no control over these cookies and they are not related to tracking or advertising.

Service Purpose
Google Fonts CDN Serves Oswald and Archivo Narrow typefaces. May cache resources in your browser for performance. No personal data collected by Griffix Racing through this service.
Tailwind CSS CDN Delivers the Tailwind CSS framework stylesheet. May cache the file in your browser. No tracking involved.

For information on how these third-party services handle data, please refer to Google's and Tailwind's respective privacy policies.

4. MANAGING COOKIES AND LOCAL STORAGE

You have full control over the data stored in your browser. To remove all Griffix Racing site data, including your saved cart:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data → Select "Cookies and other site data" and "Cached images and files"
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data → Remove All
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data

Note that clearing your browser's local storage will empty your shopping cart. Your order history is not stored in the browser and will not be affected.

Because we use no tracking or advertising cookies, blocking cookies in your browser will not significantly affect your experience on this site — only cart persistence will be impacted.
