Step by Step

INSTALLATION GUIDE

01

CLEAN THE PLASTIC

Degrease all panels with isopropyl alcohol. Remove any old graphics, wax, or silicone spray. The surface must be completely bare and dry before application.

02

WARM THE GRAPHIC

Use a heat gun or hair dryer to warm the graphic to approximately 40°C. This activates the adhesive and makes the vinyl flexible for curved surfaces.

03

PEEL AND POSITION

Peel the backing paper slowly at 180°. Start from one corner and work outward. Use transfer tape to hold the graphic while you position it on the panel.

04

SQUEEGEE AND SET

Work from the centre outward with a squeegee or credit card to remove air bubbles. Apply heat again and press the edges firmly. Leave 24 hours before riding.
