Our Story

FORGED IN DIRT.
PROVEN IN
BATTLE.

Griffix Racing was built by riders who were fed up with generic graphics that didn't survive the first river crossing. We design from the seat of the bike — understanding what it means to take a hit, drag through roots, and still need your machine looking fresh on Sunday.

Every kit is printed on 3M adhesive stock with military-grade laminate. UV resistant. Chemical resistant. Scratch resistant. Built to last a full season in the gnarliest conditions on earth.
