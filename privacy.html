POLICY

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: February 2026. This policy applies to all information collected through the Griffix Racing website and any related services.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you place an order or contact us, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Full name and email address
  • Phone number (optional, for delivery queries)
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Order history and product preferences
  • Payment method selection (PayPal, Venmo, or Zelle / bank transfer)

We do not store payment card numbers, account credentials, or any sensitive financial data directly. All payment is processed through the respective third-party platforms.

We collect only what we need to fulfil your order and provide support. Nothing more.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Processing and fulfilling your order
  • Sending order confirmation and payment instructions by email
  • Providing shipping tracking information
  • Responding to enquiries and support requests
  • Improving our website, products, and services over time

We do not use your information for any purpose beyond what is necessary to deliver the service you've requested.

3. THIRD-PARTY PAYMENT PROCESSING

We accept payment via PayPal, Venmo, and Zelle / bank transfer. When you choose one of these methods, you are directed to complete payment through that platform's own secure interface. Griffix Racing does not handle, store, or have access to your account credentials or card details at any point.

Each payment platform has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review the relevant policy for the method you choose:

  • PayPal Privacy Policy
  • Venmo Privacy Policy
  • Zelle Privacy Policy

4. EMAIL COMMUNICATIONS

Order confirmation and payment instruction emails are sent automatically when you place an order. These are transactional communications required to fulfil your purchase and cannot be opted out of while an order is active.

We may send marketing emails (new products, promotions, content) only if you have explicitly opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of any marketing email, or by emailing privacy@griffixracing.com.au.

5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

Your data is stored securely on our servers with access limited to authorised Griffix Racing staff only. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or misuse.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. The only third parties who may receive your data are shipping providers who need your address to deliver your order.

6. COOKIES

We use essential cookies and browser localStorage to maintain your shopping cart session between page visits. No advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies are set by Griffix Racing. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, or to ask any questions about how your data is handled, contact us at:

privacy@griffixracing.com.au

We will respond to all privacy requests within 14 business days.

8. CONTACT

For all privacy-related enquiries, requests, or concerns, contact us at privacy@griffixracing.com.au. We take data privacy seriously and will always handle your enquiry with care and discretion.

Griffix Racing reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated via email to registered customers. Continued use of this website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
